## Project Overview
This project analyzes retail sales transaction data to uncover **monthly sales trends, seasonality patterns, revenue concentration across products and categories, regional performance differences**, and **sales growth (MoM & YoY)**.

The objective is to transform raw transactional data into **actionable business insights** that support inventory planning, marketing strategy, and regional optimization.

---

## Business Questions
- How do monthly sales evolve over time?
- Is there clear seasonality in demand?
- Which products and categories drive the majority of revenue?
- Are certain regions consistently underperforming?
- Is the business demonstrating sustainable growth?

---

## Dataset
- Source: Superstore Sales Dataset  
- Size: ~9,800 transaction records  
- Key fields: Order Date, Sales, Product, Category, Region

---

## Analysis Summary
- Sales show **clear seasonality**, with stronger performance toward year-end.
- Revenue is **highly concentrated** among a small group of top products.
- Some categories and regions underperform relative to the overall average.
- Both **MoM and YoY growth metrics indicate an overall upward trend**.
